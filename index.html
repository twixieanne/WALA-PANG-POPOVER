Like a Naiad guarding the rivers of ancient Greece, Toni flows through the challenges of school with grace and determination. In mythology, Naiads were known to bring life and resilience to the lands they touched, and Toni mirrors this energy, arming herself with knowledge and perseverance as she navigates the currents of learning. Her armor? A sharp mind and an unwavering spirit that keeps her focused on her goals.
Trixie embodies the spirit of the Dryads, the nymphs of the trees, deeply rooted yet reaching ever higher. In Greek mythology, Dryads were guardians of the forest, protecting and nurturing their surroundings. Like them, Trixie thrives on growth, grounding herself in her studies while expanding her skills and wisdom. Her armor is her adaptability and creativity, which help her weather any storm and flourish in her academic journey.
Arianne channels the fiery essence of the Lampads, the nymphs who accompanied Hecate, the goddess of wisdom and magic. Known for lighting the way through the darkness, Lampads were symbols of guidance and transformation. Like them, Arianne wields her armor of curiosity and determination to shine in every challenge she faces at school, illuminating paths not just for herself but for those around her.
